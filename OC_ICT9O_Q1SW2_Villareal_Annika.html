Noli Me Tangere Characters

Image Name of the Character Description of the Character
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin He is the main character of Noli Me Tangere. His nickname is Ibarra. He studied in Europe for 7 years but came back after the sudden death of his father. He is in an arranged marriage with Maria Clara.
Maria Clara Maria Clara is the female love interest of Ibarra. She is always described as a beautiful woman. She is also a strong-willed woman.
Don Santiago Kapitan Tiago is the father of Maria Clara. He is often referred to as Kapitan Tiago. He is religious and is known as a rich and kind person by the people of San Diego.
Tiya Isabel Tiya Isabel is a cousin of Kapitan Tiago. Since Maria Clara's mom passed away early, she was the one who took care of Maria Clara. In the story, she is always seen together with Maria Clara.
Padre Damaso Padre Damaso is a Franciscan Priest. He is known as a self-righteous personality. He is a representation of the priests back when the Spanish colonized us. It is later revealed that he is Maria Clara's real father.
